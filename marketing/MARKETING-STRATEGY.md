# CoachPilot Marketing Strategy

**Goal:** Get CoachPilot in front of volunteer youth coaches. Drive traffic to coachpilot.org/plans.
**Timeline:** 90 days to meaningful traction. No shortcuts.
**Budget:** $0. Organic only until proven.

---

## What the Data Says

- Posts with 40-80 characters get 66% more engagement than long posts
- Posts over 280 characters see significantly lower engagement
- Images lead Facebook engagement at 5.2%
- Reels get 22% more engagement and 50% distribution boost over standard posts
- Shares and saves are more powerful signals than likes or comments
- Responding to comments within the first hour boosts algorithm distribution
- 80% of content should be value. 20% promotional.
- Facebook Groups are the #1 organic distribution channel for pages with zero followers
- Consistency (3-5 posts/week) for 60-90 days before expecting meaningful growth

Sources: SocialPilot, Buffer (45M+ posts analyzed), Brand24, HookAgency, Metricool, PostEverywhere

---

## What We Did Wrong (5 Posts Published 2026-04-08)

1. **Too long.** Every post was 300-500 words. Facebook users scroll past anything that needs "See more."
2. **No images.** Text-only posts get the lowest reach.
3. **No video.** Reels are the algorithm's favorite child and we posted zero.
4. **Dumped everything at once.** 5 posts in one sitting signals spam, not strategy.
5. **No distribution.** Posted to a page with 0 followers. Nobody saw them.
6. **No hooks.** The first line needs to stop the scroll. Ours didn't.

---

## The Fix: 3 Phase Strategy

### Phase 1: Foundation (Week 1)

**Page Setup:**
- Cover photo (use Canva: "Free Practice Plans for 16 Sports" graphic)
- Profile photo (CP logo, already set)
- About section: "Free tools for volunteer youth coaches. Practice plans for 16 sports. Built by a coach who gets it."
- Website link: coachpilot.org
- CTA button: "Learn More" -> coachpilot.org/plans

**Clean Up Existing Posts:**
- Delete 3 of the 5 dumped posts. Keep Post 1 (intro) and Post 10 (engagement question). Rewrite them shorter.
- OR: Delete all 5 and start fresh with properly formatted posts.

**Join Groups:**
Coach needs to manually join 10-15 Facebook groups:
- Youth softball coaching groups
- Youth soccer coaching groups
- Volunteer coach support groups
- Local Bonney Lake / PNW youth sports groups
- "New coach help" type groups
- General youth sports parent groups

**Note:** Share in groups as Daniel (personal profile), linking back to the CoachPilot page. Personal shares get more reach than page shares in groups.

### Phase 2: Content Engine (Weeks 2-4)

**Daily Posting Framework (pick 3-4 days/week to start):**

| Time | Format | Type | Example |
|------|--------|------|---------|
| Morning | Reel (30-60s) | Value/Tips | Coach records using TikTok scripts |
| Midday | Image + short caption | Relatable/Funny | Meme or designed graphic |
| Evening | Text post (short) | Engagement/Question | "What's the hardest drill to teach 6-year-olds?" |

**Post Format Rules:**
1. First line = scroll-stopper hook. Make them stop.
2. Keep it under 100 words. Under 50 is better.
3. Every post needs an image, video, or designed graphic. No naked text.
4. Line breaks after every 1-2 sentences. White space.
5. End with a question or CTA. Never both.
6. Link in comments, not in the post (algorithm penalizes external links in post body).

**Distribution:**
- Post to page first
- Then share the best 2-3 posts per week into relevant Facebook Groups
- Share as Daniel (personal), not as CoachPilot page
- Add a personal comment when sharing: "Hey, I built this for coaches like us. Thought it might help."
- When people react to group posts, invite them to follow the CoachPilot page

### Phase 3: Scale (Weeks 5-12)

- Analyze which posts got the most engagement
- Double down on those formats
- Start Instagram (repurpose best-performing Facebook content)
- TikTok if Coach can commit to recording 2-3 videos per week
- Consider $5-10/day Facebook boost on top-performing posts
- Email list building via lead magnet on coachpilot.org

## Reels/Video Priority List (Coach needs to record)

These are the highest-value videos. 30-60 seconds each. Shoot on phone, be authentic.

1. "Your first practice is tomorrow" (Script 1) - HIGHEST PRIORITY
2. "You got voluntold to coach" (Script 7) - relatable, shareable
3. "Nobody tells volunteer coaches this" (Script 4) - emotional, shareable
4. "The #1 mistake new coaches make" (Script 2) - educational
5. "You're doing a great job, Coach" (Script 10) - emotional closer

**Video tips:**
- First 3 seconds = hook. Start mid-thought, not with "Hey guys"
- Talk to camera like you're talking to one coach
- Natural lighting, outside on a field is perfect
- Text overlay on screen (add in CapCut or TikTok editor)
- Post as Reel on Facebook AND as TikTok AND as Instagram Reel (triple distribution)

## Metrics to Track (Weekly)

- Page followers (goal: 100 in 30 days, 500 in 90 days)
- Post reach (especially group shares vs page posts)
- Website clicks to coachpilot.org/plans
- Comments and shares (more valuable than likes)
- Best performing post format (image vs video vs text)

---

## Key Principle

**We are not a brand posting content. We are a coach helping coaches.**

Every post should feel like advice from a friend, not marketing from a company. Daniel's personal voice IS the brand. Authenticity beats production quality every time.
