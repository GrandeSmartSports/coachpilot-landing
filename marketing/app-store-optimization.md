## ASO Research Notes

**Top competitor:** Coach One - Practice Plans ($5.99/wk or $59.99/yr)
- Their title: "Coach One - Practice Plans"
- Their subtitle: "AI Sports Coaching Assistant"
- Weaknesses: doesn't list sport count, no drill count in description, copy-paste errors, collects user data

**Our advantages to emphasize:**
- 16 sports (they don't list a number)
- 5,798 drills (specific number builds trust)
- Half the price ($29.99/yr vs $59.99/yr)
- No data collection / privacy-first
- Built by a real coach (credibility)
- Free web version (they don't have one)
- "Voluntold" language resonates with target audience

**Keyword gaps to target:**
- "practice plan generator" — low competition, high intent
- "youth coaching app" — moderate competition
- "volunteer coach" — very low competition, perfect audience
- "first practice" — low competition, high desperation (our buyer)
